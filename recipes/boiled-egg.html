Boiled Egg Recipe


Description

A boiled egg a day keeps the hunger away. Lots of protein in there.

Ingredients

  • Egg or eggs

Step 1: Place eggs into a saucepan and fill with water until eggs are just barely covered.

Step 2: Bring water to a boil. Boil eggs for 4 1/2 minutes. Remove from heat and let eggs sit in hot water for 20 minutes.

Step 3: Transfer eggs to a bowl and either peel and serve, or keep shells on and place in the refrigerator until you are ready to serve.
